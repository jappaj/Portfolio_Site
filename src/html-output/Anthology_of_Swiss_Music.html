Anthology of Swiss Music
  The Anthology of Swiss Music is a motion graphic based on a poster by the same name from Josef Muller Brockmann, set to the music of a traditional Swiss string quartet. The original poster is based on the form of a vinyl LP, and so in creating my animated tribute, I sought to reference the movement of a turntable as an alternative way to explore the forms of the illustration. The animation relied heavily on the three-dimensional camera controls of Adobe AfterEffects, and served as a healthy learning experience for the complexities of organic camera movement in a digital environment.
